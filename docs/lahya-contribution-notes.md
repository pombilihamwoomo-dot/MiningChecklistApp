## App Understanding

MiningChecklistApp supports a mining safety workflow with two main roles:

- Workers start a shift and submit pre-shift safety checklist items.
- Supervisors review submitted checklist records and identify incomplete or missing submissions.

## Testing Notes

Recommended checks for demonstration:

- Register or log in as a worker.
- Start a shift before opening the safety checklist.
- Submit a complete checklist and confirm it appears in the worker history.
- Submit an incomplete checklist and confirm it is marked as incomplete.
- Log in as a supervisor and confirm worker submissions appear in the supervisor view.
